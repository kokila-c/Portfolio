About me

Welcome to my corner of the web! I'm on an exciting journey as a student delving into the world of web development, eager to craft beautiful and functional websites that leave a lasting impact. With a passion for learning and a penchant for creativity, I strive to create digital experiences that not only dazzle the eyes but also serve a meaningful purpose.
In my approach to both life and work, I embody independence, service, and solutions. Armed with effective communication skills and a knack for problem-solving, I'm always ready to lend a helping hand wherever it's needed. My academic background spans both information technology and business administration, providing me with a well-rounded skill set to tackle diverse challenges.
Originally from India and now proudly calling Norway my home since 2012. Fluent in Norsk(B1/B2-nivå), English, and Tamil, with a knack for understanding other Indian regional languages, I find joy in bridging linguistic divides and fostering connections.
Beyond the digital realm, I embrace a diverse range of interests and pursuits. As a mother of two, family holds a special place in my heart, grounding me in purpose and love. In my leisure time, you'll often find me immersed in badminton,zumba, arts and crafts, or a gripping historical fiction novel. And as an avid gardener, I have a green thumb for nurturing plants, particularly edible and indian or tropical varieties.
Join me on this exhilarating journey of discovery and creation, as we weave together technology, creativity, and culture to shape a brighter digital future.
